Nova Scotia Folk Art Festival: Media

In the past few years I have been increaing my involvement with the Nova Scotia Folk Art Festival as the Social Media Manager. The Nova Scotia Folk Art Festival is a non-profit festival that takes place each year in Nova Scotia. At the festival, I take photos, videos and manage the posting and promotion of the festival online.
