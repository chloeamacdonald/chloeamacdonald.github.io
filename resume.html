About


Chloe is a New Media Artist from New Brunswick, Canada, with a background shaped by formal studies at Toronto Metropolitan University. Her practice explores the intersection of tangible interactive art and cultural heritage, creating immersive experiences that connect audiences with stories of place and identity. She strives to create work that displays community heritage through tech-integrated platforms. She draws inspiration from both maritime themes and community to build her pieces. Chloe also enjoys the outdoors and cooking, but she adores creating art!
